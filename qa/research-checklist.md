# Research Checklist

- [ ] Research question is explicit and scoped.
- [ ] Inclusion and exclusion criteria are documented.
- [ ] Source discovery method is recorded.
- [ ] Important claims have source IDs.
- [ ] Important claims have evidence IDs.
- [ ] Facts, inferences, hypotheses, and recommendations are labeled.
- [ ] Synthesis is separated from raw notes.
- [ ] Final output receives independent quality review.
