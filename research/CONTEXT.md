# Research Context

## One-Line Goal

Research two proposal tracks: topic-aware compaction for multi-topic LLM agent sessions, and contract-driven agent harness engineering for cross-model capability-gap compression.

## Research Question

How should each proposal be framed, evidenced, and tested so it can become a credible academic or systems research project?

## Scope

Included: local proposal materials, existing experiment reports, source/evidence tracking, ablation design, benchmark design, and next-step paper planning.

Excluded: final claims of statistical significance, final literature review completeness, and unverified cross-model conclusions.

## Method

Use the installed research workflow: brief framing, local source inspection, external source positioning, evidence ledger construction, synthesis, report writing, quality review, and method/protocol design.

## Evidence Rules

- Use source IDs for all important references.
- Use evidence IDs for claims that enter synthesis or reports.
- Separate extracted facts, inferred conclusions, ambiguous findings, and proposed recommendations.
