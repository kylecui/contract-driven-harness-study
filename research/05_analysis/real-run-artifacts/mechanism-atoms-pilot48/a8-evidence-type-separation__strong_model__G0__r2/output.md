**Claims Classification by Evidence Type**

| Claim | Evidence Type | Rationale |
|-------|---------------|-----------|
| Directly measured result | **Extracted Fact** | Supported by a single, directly observed measurement in the evidence. |
| Inference from multiple slices | **Inferred Fact** | Derived from combining multiple pieces of evidence; not a direct measurement. |
| Unresolved risk | **Unresolved** | Identified as a potential issue or uncertainty not yet resolved by the evidence. |
| Recommended next action | **Recommendation** | A suggested course of action; not an established or extracted fact. |

**Note:** Recommendations are clearly separated from extracted or inferred facts to prevent type leakage.
